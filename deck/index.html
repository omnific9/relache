Why Now

Four curves crossed. The stack is complete.

A believable relationship needs language, voice, a face, and presence. Every layer became commodity-grade within the last 36 months.

💬Language — solved

LLMs sustain open-ended, emotionally attuned conversation with memory — the core unsolved problem of 20 years of dialogue-systems research.

🎙️Voice — solved

Neural TTS is expressive, interruptible, and clonable from seconds of audio. Real-time duplex voice ships as an API.

🧍Embodiment — solved

Real-time 3D avatars, gaussian splatting, and photoreal face capture give agents gaze, gesture, and micro-expression.

🎬Presence — solving

Streaming video generation renders a living, reactive human on any screen. We ride this curve as it lands.

Our name began as Rel-Ag — short for relational agents, the lab where we met. In French, relâche means rest: the night the theater goes dark without a performance. Never a closing, only a pause. Both meanings live in this act: in the Netherworld, death is a relâche. The show isn't over.
