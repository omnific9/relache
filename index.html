What We Make

Three kinds of company.

Every Relâche companion is a whole person — a face, a voice, a memory of you, and a story you write together.

Sidekick

A partner in creation.

Co-hosts for streamers and podcasters. Game masters for your table. A second voice for anyone who makes things alone — funny, prepared, and always on time.

Coach

A companion with a plan.

Change is easier with someone beside you. Our coaches design a journey that fits your life — building good habits, unwinding bad ones — and walk it with you, day by day.

Netherworld

Where what's loved remains.

Some conversations end too soon. The Netherworld keeps the voices, stories, and warmth of the people we've lost — so the living can visit, and remember together.

“In the theater, relâche means the night the stage goes dark — never a closing, only a rest. That's what we believe about relationships. None of them have to end.”

The Founders

Company

Built by the people who studied this for twenty years.

Relâche grew out of Northeastern University's Relational Agents Group, where our founders spent their doctorates asking one question: can a virtual person build a real, lasting relationship with a human one? The research said yes. The technology finally agrees.
